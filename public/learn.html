How it Works

We believe that learning is done most effectively when it is collaborative. Our new accelerator programs focus on teaching students by having them build projects together once they learn the necessary concepts. By forming groups of 4 - 6 and signing up for our accelerators, your child will not only learn the basics of a field of computer science but also have the skills to turn their own ideas into real products and be ready for a future in computer science. Our program spends the first three days teaching essential concepts that surpass an intro class in coding. The next two days are then spent building collaborative projects to reinforce the skills learned.

Curriculum

Our accelerators are currently either teaching front-end development or object-oriented programming. Through the front-end development accelerator, your child will learn how to build interactive websites that change based on a user's needs. We teach HTML, CSS, and Javascript in ordre to create these websites. In addition, we focus on responsive web design and the importance of visual design when building projects. Our object-oriented accelerator follows the traditional path of coding through Python, where students learn the fundamentals of data structures, classes, and objects. These concepts are then used to build scalable projects that follow proper object-oriented principles.

Logistics

In order to join an accelerator, you must sign up with a group of 4 - 6 students. Once you have a group ready, simply reach out to us with the form below. We are available to run accelerators any week with the program running from 9am - 3pm every week day. The accelerator program is priced at $500 per student, but we are currently offering a price of $400 per student if they sign up with a complete group of 4 - 6 students. We also offer a free workshop for individual students or groups if you are unsure about our curriculum or would like to meet with our tutors first.
